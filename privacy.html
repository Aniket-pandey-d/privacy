Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("Arrow") requests and uses access to your device’s microphone.

1. Purpose of Microphone Access

Our App requires access to your device’s microphone solely to enable essential features such as:

  • Playing interactive sound effects during gameplay.
  • Enabling audio-related functionality.
  • Improving your experience by supporting sound playback features.

We do NOT record, capture, store, or collect any audio from your device. Microphone access is used only to play sounds and enhance app functionality.

2. What Data is Collected

  • The App does not capture or record any audio input.
  • No audio data is stored or transmitted externally.
  • All audio processing occurs locally on your device.

3. User Consent

By granting microphone access, you allow the App to enable these sound-related features. If you deny permission, some audio features may not function.

4. Data Security and Storage

  • We do not collect or store any personal audio data.
  • Microphone usage is limited to real-time sound playback within the App.
  • We follow best security practices to protect your device while using the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not share microphone data with any third parties or advertisers.

Should this policy change, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you accordingly.

6. User Rights

  • You may revoke or disable microphone access anytime via your device settings.
  • You can contact us for any questions or concerns about your privacy.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Please review it periodically.
